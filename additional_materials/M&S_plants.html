Green and leafy or covered in colourful blooms, our plants brighten your day. However large or compact your home, there’s always space for more greenery. We’ve got outdoor plants for your beds, pots and window boxes, plus indoor plants to liven up any living space, kitchen or even bathroom.

Whether you have a small balcony or a rambling garden, we’ve got inspiring flowering plants , shrubs and trees to bring your vision to life. Flex those green fingers by planting trees, verdant bushes and sowing bulbs for the seasons ahead, or opt for a handy ready-made planter filled with a curated selection of flowers to create instant colour. You’ll find shade-loving beauties and sunshine-lovers for light-filled patios, plus hardy varieties that are ready for whatever the British winter has to offer.

For your indoor spaces, choose from vibrant orchids and roses or ferns and succulents . Green plants give your room a calming, jungle-like atmosphere. You can tailor your aesthetic by choosing the perfect plant pots or planters to suit your space – quality ceramic pots are a timeless choice, made to last. Whatever the occasion, greenery-loving friends and loved ones will always appreciate a plant for their collection. Browse plant gift bundles for every season, complete with a coordinating pot and cute extras like chocolate or some bubbly, or pick out one of our pre-planted troughs or pots for a thoughtful present.
